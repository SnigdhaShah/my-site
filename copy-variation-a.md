## ABOUT

**Section Headline:**
How I got here — and why it matters to you

**Body:**
I didn't start in the social sector. I started in corporate finance — M&A, investor relations, strategic planning. That world shaped how I think: rigorously, structurally, always with an eye on long-term value.

Then, in what felt like happenstance at the time, I found myself in philanthropy. And I stayed — for fifteen years. Eight of those as Founding CEO of SVP India – Kolkata Chapter, where I built an engaged donor network from the ground up, structured grant-making partnerships, and learned what it really means to build something that outlasts you.

Connecting The Dots was founded in 2026 because I kept seeing the same gap: people and organisations with genuine intent and real resources — but without the strategy, the structure, or the right connections to make their giving work. I've been on both sides of that gap. I know what's missing. And I know how to build the bridge.

## WHAT I OFFER

**Section Headline:**
What I bring to your work

**Intro:**
I don't offer off-the-shelf packages. I bring five capabilities — applied to whatever challenge you're navigating, whatever stage you're at.

---

**Strategic Clarity**
When the landscape is complex and the path is unclear, I help you think through it — structuring the problem, naming what matters, and building a direction you can act on with confidence.

---

**Milestone-Based Planning**
Good intentions need a plan that holds. I turn vision into executable, time-bound roadmaps — with clear milestones, defined ownership, and a logic built to survive real-world complexity.

---

**Team Building**
The right work needs the right people. I help you identify what you need, find who fits, and build teams capable of carrying the work forward — not just at launch, but over time.

---

**Network & Resource Connection**
Fifteen years in India's social sector means I know who's who — donors, foundations, nonprofits, and organisations doing the work that matters. I make the introductions that open the right doors, at the right moment.

**Capacity Building**
For organisations ready to grow, I work on the foundations — governance, fundraising, measurement, and the structural rigour that lets good work scale without losing its integrity.
