3. **Cost and engagement are invisible.** This is a trust killer. I'm not asking for exact pricing, but transparency on ballpark investment and typical timeline removes friction. Right now, I'm guessing, and I might guess "too expensive."

4. **CSR feels like an afterthought.** The site says you work with CSR companies, but the proof and client list suggest foundations are your real strength. If CSR is secondary, say so. If it's primary, *prove* it with a CSR client story.

5. **The form is a black hole.** There's no indication of what happens after I submit. Do I get a call? An email proposal? A questionnaire? A fee? This uncertainty will stop me from filling it out.
