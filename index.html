What I bring —
and why it's different

Most advisors in this space come from one world. I come from two.

My career began in corporate finance — M&A, investor relations, strategic planning. That world shaped how I think: rigorously, structurally, always with an eye on long-term value.

Then, in what felt like happenstance at the time, I found myself in philanthropy. And I stayed — for fifteen years. Eight of those as Founding CEO of SVP India – Kolkata Chapter. SVP — Social Venture Partners — is the venture philanthropy model: engaged donors, long-term nonprofit partnerships, embedded strategic support. Building the Kolkata chapter from scratch gave me something rare: a clear view from both sides of the giving relationship.

Connecting The Dots was founded in 2026 because I kept seeing the same gap. India's CSR mandate reached ₹35,000 crore in 2024 — and yet companies working through NGO partners dropped from nearly 80% to under 30% in four years. More giving, less reaching. People and organisations with genuine intent and real resources, without the strategy, the structure, or the right connections to make it work. I've been on both sides of that gap. I know what's missing. And I know how to build the bridge.

Six capabilities.
Applied to your work.

I work on the problems that sit between intention and execution — the strategy that isn't clear, the India context that's missing, the narrative that hasn't been written, and the connections that aren't yet in place.

01

Philanthropy Strategy & Foundation Building

Whether you're designing your first giving framework or building a five-year foundation strategy, I develop the governance, theory of change, and advisory structures that make philanthropic intent durable — and defensible.

02

Milestone-Based Planning

Good intentions need a plan that holds. I turn vision into executable, time-bound roadmaps — with clear milestones, defined ownership, and a logic built to survive real-world complexity.

03

India Context Advisory

India's social sector is navigated, not entered. I provide the on-ground knowledge that makes giving viable here — NGO due diligence, FCRA and compliance mapping, budget benchmarking, ecosystem navigation, and leadership hiring support.

04

Network & Resource Connection

Fifteen years in India's social sector means I know who's who — donors, foundations, nonprofits, and organisations doing the work that matters. I make the introductions that open the right doors, at the right moment.

05

Capacity Building & CSR Alignment

For NGOs and CSR teams ready to scale, I work on the organizational foundations — governance, fundraising, impact measurement, and the structural rigour that lets good work grow without losing its integrity.

06

Narrative & Proposal Development

The strongest work often loses funding because it hasn't been articulated well. I help organisations and CSR teams craft proposals, annual narratives, and positioning that reflects both their authenticity and the rigour funders need to say yes.
